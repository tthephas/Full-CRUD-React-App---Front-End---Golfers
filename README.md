# Full-CRUD-React-App---Front-End---Golfers

Full CRUD app with the subject being Professional Golfers. 

Give users the ability to go in and CREATE a golfer of their choice and give them statistics. 

Users can give golfers a name, the tour they play on, and how many years they have been on the tour. Users can then give their golfers stats, like wins, losses, and career earnings. 

Users can update and delete these traits and stats as they please. 


#Technology used
Javascript
React
Boostrap
